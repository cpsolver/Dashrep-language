<h3>Action snippets</h3>

<p>Reminder:&nbsp; Actions that process <i>words</i> also can process numbers and lists of numbers.&nbsp; Consider a phrase that contains <i>12 3.4 -0.5</i>. This phrase can be used with <i>numeric</i> actions that recognize them as numbers and do numeric calculations.&nbsp; Yet also the numbers in this phrase can be accessed using word-oriented actions, where each number is handled as a space-delimited word.</p>
