Action snippets

Reminder:  Actions that process words also can process numbers and lists of numbers.  Consider a phrase that contains 12 3.4 -0.5. This phrase can be used with numeric actions that recognize them as numbers and do numeric calculations.  Yet also the numbers in this phrase can be accessed using word-oriented actions, where each number is handled as a space-delimited word.
